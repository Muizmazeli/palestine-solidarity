Key Facts About Palestine

Population

Over 5 million Palestinians live in the West Bank and Gaza Strip.

Occupation

Palestinian territories have been under Israeli occupation since 1967.

Refugees

More than 7 million Palestinians are refugees worldwide.

Human Rights

Numerous UN resolutions have condemned human rights violations in Palestine.
